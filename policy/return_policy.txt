AcmeCorp Return & Refund Policy

1. Returns are accepted within 30 days of the original purchase date.
2. Items must be in their original condition and include all packaging.
3. Proof of purchase is required for any return or refund.
4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.
5. Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the item was defective or incorrect.
6. Final sale items, digital downloads, and gift cards are non-refundable.
7. Refunds are not issued for damage caused by misuse, neglect, or unauthorized modifications.
8. All returns are subject to inspection. Approval is at the discretion of AcmeCorp staff.
